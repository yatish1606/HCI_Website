Great place to spend vacation

I stayed in this property for 1 night but it was so so peaceful that I decided to extend my trip by another day. This property is small but has ample places to relax yourself - pool, swings outside rooms, hammocks. Rooms are spacious. If you stay in first floor, you’d enjoy sitting in balcony all day. GF rooms have a swing outside each room. I wouldn’t recommend pool facing cottages as they are small and very close to pool so if you don’t have occupied all the cottages , privacy would be a concern. I really loved the food and the senior who everyday delighted us with his recommendations. He made sure my 1 year kid is getting the kind of food he wouldn’t fuss about.
